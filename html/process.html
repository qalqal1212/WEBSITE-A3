PROCESS —

1

DISCOVERY & BRIEFING

Each project begins with a conversation to understand the client’s goals, audience, and creative needs.

2

RESEARCH & CONCEPT DEVELOPMENT

I collect inspiration, explore design directions, and build a mood board or draft concepts to guide the project.

3

DEPOSIT & AGREEMENT

Before design work starts, a 50% deposit is required to confirm the project and secure the timeline. This ensures mutual commitment and clarity.

4

DESIGN & CREATION

Initial designs, animations, or visuals are produced based on the agreed concept. Drafts are shared for feedback.

5

REFINEMENT & FEEDBACK

Feedback rounds are included, with revisions made to ensure the final design aligns with the client’s vision.

6

FINAL DELIVERY & PAYMENT

The remaining 50% is paid upon approval, after which final files are delivered in the required formats.
